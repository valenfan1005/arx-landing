ARX Trading Glossary

Short, fact-anchored definitions of the perpetual trading terms used across ARX content. Each entry has its own page with full FAQ and citations. Use the index as a quick reference when reading the blog or insights.

Glossary
Funding Rate (Perpetual)

A periodic payment between perpetual contract longs and shorts that keeps the perp price tracking the underlying spot price.
Glossary
HIP-3

Hyperliquid's permissionless market listing standard for perpetual contracts.
Glossary
RWA Perpetuals

Perpetual futures contracts on real-world assets (gold, oil, equities) that settle on-chain.
Glossary
On-Chain Copy Trading

Automatically mirroring another wallet's trades on a transparent blockchain, with self-custody and verifiable track records.
