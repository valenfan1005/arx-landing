Everything you need to know about trading RWA and crypto perps on ARX.

RWA (real-world asset) perpetuals let you trade gold, oil, S&P 500, and other traditional assets as on-chain perpetual contracts. Unlike CFD brokers, there are no overnight fees, no counterparty dealing desk, and you keep full custody of your funds. ARX provides access to these markets via Hyperliquid.
ARX is a trading frontend built on Hyperliquid. Connect your wallet, choose your market — gold, oil, S&P 500, or crypto perps — and trade directly on-chain. ARX adds smart signals with regime detection, confluence scoring, and optional copy trading so you can follow top-performing wallets automatically.
Overnight fees (swap rates) are daily charges CFD brokers apply to open positions — often 0.01-0.03% per day, which compounds to 5-10% annually. On-chain perpetuals on Hyperliquid use transparent funding rates instead, which are market-driven and often near zero. No dealing desk, no hidden costs.
ARX is fully non-custodial — we never hold your funds or private keys. All trades execute through Hyperliquid's on-chain infrastructure, so your assets remain in your wallet at all times. All trading carries risk: leveraged perps can lead to significant losses. ARX provides risk controls like position sizing and max drawdown limits to help you manage exposure.
