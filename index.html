Everything you need to know about DeFi copy trading with ARX.

DeFi copy trading lets you automatically replicate the trades of top-performing wallets directly on-chain — no centralized exchange, no middlemen. Unlike traditional copy trading on CEXs, your funds stay in your own wallet the entire time. ARX monitors elite traders on Hyperliquid and mirrors their positions into your account with full transparency.
ARX combines three layers of intelligence: wallet tracking to identify consistently profitable traders, regime detection to understand current market conditions, and confluence scoring to filter signals by quality. When a high-conviction trade is detected, ARX can automatically execute it on your behalf on Hyperliquid — with your own risk parameters.
ARX is fully non-custodial — we never hold your funds or private keys. All trades execute through Hyperliquid's on-chain infrastructure, so your assets remain in your wallet at all times. That said, all trading carries risk: past performance of copied wallets doesn't guarantee future results, and leveraged perps can lead to significant losses. ARX provides risk controls like position sizing and max drawdown limits to help you manage exposure.
Market regime detection classifies the current market into states like Trending, Range-Bound, Compression, Transition, or Crisis. Different trading strategies work in different regimes — trend-following in a ranging market is a recipe for losses. ARX uses regime awareness to filter copy signals, so you're only following trades suited to current conditions. Learn more about the 5 regimes.
